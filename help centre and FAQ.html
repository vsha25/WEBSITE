Help Center & FAQ

Find answers to common questions

How do I register as a service provider?

To register as a service provider, go to the "Sign Up" page and choose "Service Provider" as your role.

How do I book a service?

Search for a service, click on the provider, and use the "Book Now" button to schedule an appointment.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and online banking.

Can I cancel a booked service?

Yes, you can cancel within 24 hours of booking without any charges.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the "Contact Us" page or email us at support@localsdirectory.com.
